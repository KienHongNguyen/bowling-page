CLASSES

BEGINNER

Perfect for those new to bowling, our beginner classes teach the fundamentals, including stance, approach, and release. You'll learn to select the right ball and adjust your aim and speed. These fun classes provide practice and personalized feedback.

INTERMEDIATE

Take your bowling to the next level with our intermediate classes. Refine your technique, improve your accuracy, and increase your consistency. Learn strategies for targeting spares and adjusting to lane conditions. Our instructors provide in-depth analysis and drills to help you improve.

ADVANCED

Designed for serious bowlers, our advanced classes focus on mastering the mental game, fine-tuning your release, and optimizing ball dynamics. Learn to read lane conditions, make strategic adjustments, and perform under pressure.

ABOUT US

Established in 2012, our modern bowling alley in Wangaratta offers a top-tier entertainment experience. With 12 lanes and a vibrant atmosphere, we cater to all ages and skill levels. Perfect for birthdays, family outings, and corporate events, our glass-walled party room and all-in-one packages guarantee a memorable time. Conveniently located within the Wangaratta Co-Store Complex, we offer nearby shopping and dining options with ample parking. Our dedicated staff ensures an exceptional experience for seasoned bowlers and first-timers alike. Discover why we're Wangaratta's premier bowling destination!
